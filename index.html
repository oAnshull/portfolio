About

Technical work,
presented clearly.

I'm Anshul, a software developer based in India. Most of my work is centered on automation, backend integrations, low-level systems, and tools that solve practical problems.

I like projects that require both implementation and investigation - whether that's building APIs, working with rendering systems, designing bots, or understanding how software behaves under the hood.

I care about writing software that is reliable, maintainable, and clear in purpose. Good presentation matters, but the engineering has to hold up first.

I also use agentic coding tools and MCP-based workflows to speed up research, implementation, debugging, and repetitive engineering tasks without losing sight of the underlying system.
